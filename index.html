My UX Skills

User Research

Conducting user research to understand behaviors, needs, and motivations through methods such as interviews, surveys, usability testing, and observations. This ensures that design solutions are user-centered and meet the actual needs of the target audience.

Wireframing and Prototyping

Creating wireframes and developing interactive prototypes using tools like Sketch, Figma, or Adobe XD. This includes testing user flows, iterating designs based on feedback, and collaborating with stakeholders to refine prototypes.

Information Architecture

Designing site maps, navigation structures, and user flows to ensure content is organized intuitively. This involves conducting card sorting exercises, creating clear content hierarchy, and ensuring logical taxonomy.

Visual Identity

Crafting cohesive visual identities that include the design of logos, typography, color palettes, and brand guidelines. This ensures a consistent and engaging visual presence across all platforms, enhancing brand recognition and user experience.
